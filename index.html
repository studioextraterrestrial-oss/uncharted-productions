— our mission

It's Neo-African Cinema.

We're here to take Nollywood to its peak, where our films meet a global standard of quality, earn their place on the world stage, and stand alongside the works that have gone down in history like Parasite, Pulp Fiction, City of God, The Shining.

We bring together the old and new masters of the craft to tell original stories, produce them ourselves, and support others doing the same. Stories that are culturally relevant and deeply personal. Authentic enough to inspire, to teach, and to move people the way only art can.

— our approach

What we make, and why.

i.

Deeply personal, original films

Stories that mean something to the filmmaker and to the audience. Sometimes for millions, sometimes for a few. Films that move deeply, that touch identity, humanity, society and nature, starting with the Nigerian and African experience, and let it travel from there.

ii.

Multiple genres, and new ones

Thrillers, dramas, science fiction, and the spaces in between. We also explore genre-blending in ways that challenge what audiences expect, and open story pathways Nollywood hasn't taken yet.

iii.

Culturally rooted, globally felt

Our films are unmistakably African, rooted in the day-to-day. But they don't translate themselves down. Lagos and London should both feel something true.

iv.

Festival-ready

Created first for home, made to live on the world stage. Cannes, Sundance, Berlin. Films in English, in native dialects, in whatever language the story calls for, held to the highest international standard.

Filmmakers as true artists.

Our Work is Made in Africa, by Africans. Written, performed, shot, and cut by people who care about every frame.

A purely human studio. We welcome new technology, international collaboration, and outside expertise where it serves the work but we never outsource the spirit of our films. AI doesn't write, direct, or edit here. Our craft is human, made by hand, by people who love this art form. We honor the traditional craft of filmmaking, and we believe that's exactly what makes our work incredibly valuable.
